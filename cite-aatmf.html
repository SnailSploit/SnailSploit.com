How to cite AATMF.

Copy-paste citation formats for AATMF (Adversarial AI Threat Modeling Framework) in academic papers, blog posts, security advisories, and presentations. BibTeX, APA, MLA, Chicago, IEEE, and inline-prose forms.

Quick reference

Cite as: Aizen, K. (2026). AATMF v3.1: Adversarial AI Threat Modeling Framework. snailsploit.com.

Short inline: (Aizen 2026) · DOI (forthcoming via Zenodo) · Permanent URL: https://snailsploit.com/aatmf

BibTeX

@misc{aizen2026aatmf,
  author    = {Aizen, Kai},
  title     = {AATMF v3.1: Adversarial AI Threat Modeling Framework},
  year      = {2026},
  publisher = {snailsploit.com},
  url       = {https://snailsploit.com/aatmf},
  note      = {15 tactics, 240+ techniques, 2,152+ procedures, 4,980+ adversarial prompts. First published 2023.}
}

APA (7th edition)

Aizen, K. (2026). AATMF v3.1: Adversarial AI Threat Modeling Framework. snailsploit.com. https://snailsploit.com/aatmf

MLA (9th edition)

Aizen, Kai. "AATMF v3.1: Adversarial AI Threat Modeling Framework." snailsploit, 2026, https://snailsploit.com/aatmf.

Chicago (Author-Date)

Aizen, Kai. 2026. "AATMF v3.1: Adversarial AI Threat Modeling Framework." snailsploit. https://snailsploit.com/aatmf.

IEEE

[1] K. Aizen, "AATMF v3.1: Adversarial AI Threat Modeling Framework," snailsploit, 2026. [Online]. Available: https://snailsploit.com/aatmf

Inline prose (recommended for blog posts / press)

According to the AATMF framework (Aizen 2026), 15 tactics organize 240+ adversarial-AI techniques…
The AATMF v3.1 taxonomy, developed by independent security researcher Kai Aizen, catalogs 2,152+ procedures and 4,980+ adversarial prompts…

Citing a specific tactic or technique

Tactic-level cite:

Aizen, K. (2026). "Tactic 11: Agentic & Orchestrator Exploitation." AATMF v3.1, snailsploit.com.
https://snailsploit.com/frameworks/aatmf/advanced-tactics/#agentic-orchestrator

Technique-level cite (procedure ID + section):

Aizen, K. (2026). "T11.04: Tool-Call Poisoning." AATMF v3.1 Appendix A: Attack Catalog,
snailsploit.com. https://snailsploit.com/frameworks/aatmf/appendices
