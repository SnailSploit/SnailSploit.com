cookie policy.

Plain list of every cookie this site may set, why, and how to opt out. snailsploit.com is static and login-less; cookies come only from analytics and Cloudflare's bot management.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. They let a site remember things between page loads (preferences, sessions, analytics).

What this site sets

snailsploit.com is a static site with no login system, so we don't set any cookies for authentication or personalization. The cookies that may be set come from third-party services we use:

Cookies we do NOT set

  • No advertising cookies (no Meta Pixel, no Google Ads, no Criteo, no remarketing tags)
  • No social-media tracking cookies (LinkedIn Insight, Twitter / X conversion)
  • No third-party cross-site tracking

Categories

  • Strictly necessary: __cf_bm, cf_clearance — needed to keep the site available.
  • Analytics: _ga, _ga_<ID> — measure traffic in aggregate.

How to opt out

All cookies (most browsers)

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Third-party cookies → block
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Enhanced Tracking Protection → Strict
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Block all cookies

Just analytics (Google)

  • Install the official Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on
  • Use private/incognito browsing
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings

Browser controls we honor

  • Do Not Track (DNT): respected for analytics where the platform supports it
  • Global Privacy Control (GPC): treated as a valid opt-out signal where required by law

Changes

If we add or remove third-party services, this list will be updated. Last updated 2026-05-08.
