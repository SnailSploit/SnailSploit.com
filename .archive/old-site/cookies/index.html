Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you interact with the site. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and tracking pixels.

Our Approach to Cookies

SnailSploit takes a privacy-first approach. We minimize the use of cookies and tracking technologies. We use Cloudflare Analytics, which is a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics solution that does not track individual users or use cookies.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

Cookie Purpose Duration
__cf_bm Cloudflare bot management 30 minutes
cf_clearance Cloudflare security challenge 1 year

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences:

Cookie Purpose Duration
theme Stores dark/light mode preference 1 year

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which does not use cookies or collect personal data. It provides aggregated, anonymized metrics about site usage without tracking individual visitors.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. If you interact with embedded content (such as YouTube videos or social media embeds), those third parties may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note that blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals. Since we use cookieless analytics, your browsing is not individually tracked regardless of your DNT setting.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices or legal requirements change. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
